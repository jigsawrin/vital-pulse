# Vital Pulse: Tactical Healer

Vital Pulse is an action-packed 2D tactical game where you take on the role of a combat healer (Sniper). Instead of dealing damage, your goal is to support your squad of AI-controlled allies by landing precise shots to restore their health and keep them in the fight.

## Features

- **Sniper-Style Healing**: Manual aiming and shooting mechanics with a 5-round ammo capacity and strategic reloading.
- **Precision Criticals**: Land headshots on allies for a "CRITICAL HEAL" bonus.
- **Dynamic AI Squad**:
  - **TANK**: Absorbs damage and guards the front line.
  - **ATTACKER**: Deals heavy damage from range.
  - **FLANKER**: Darts in and out of enemy lines for precision strikes.
- **Ultimate Abilities (ULT)**: Charge up and trigger powerful role-specific ultimates to turn the tide of battle.
- **Intense Boss Fights**: Face off against Sector Commanders every 5 waves (up to Wave 25).
- **Full Mobile Support**:
  - **Auto-Orientation**: Guides portrait users to landscape mode for the best experience.
  - **Tap Controls**: Optimized UI for mobile play, including dedicated Reload and Ultimate buttons.

## Controls

### PC (Keyboard & Mouse)
- **Mouse Movement**: Aim / Move Crosshair
- **Left Click**: Fire (Heal)
- **R Key**: Manual Reload
- **U Key**: Trigger Ready Ultimates (Allies also trigger automatically)
- **Space**: Proceed through screens (Title, Game Over, etc.)

### Mobile (Touch)
- **Tap / Drag**: Aim and Fire (Heal)
- **RELOAD Button**: Manual Reload
- **ULTIMATE Button**: Trigger Ready Ultimates
- **Anywhere Tap**: Proceed through transition screens

## Technical Details

- **Engine**: Vanilla JavaScript / HTML5 Canvas
- **Graphics**: Custom 2D sprite-based rendering
- **Audio**: Web Audio API for dynamic sound effects and BGM
